About BAFSK

Nurturing Excellence
Since 1972

Est. 1972

Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka 1206

"Shikkha · Sanjam · Shrinkhala - Education, Restraint, Discipline"

BAF Shaheen College Kurmitola runs under the direct supervision and able leadership of the Bangladesh Air Force, under the Ministry of Education of the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh and the Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Dhaka.

Established in 1972 in the beautiful environment of Dhaka Cantonment, BAFSK has become a well-reputed co-education school and college, producing leaders, scholars, and responsible citizens for the nation.
